//====The Result for every Testcase ran at 100 times=====//

Based off the testcases at different values, the policy
of attack most dangerous seems to be the most dominate 
policy winning out completely regardless of amount of 
units and enemy policy. The attack policy of attacking the
closest works extremely well with larger and very small amount of units
, while attack weakest works beter with decent size numbers.
amount of unit grow larger it is clear that attacking the 
weakest proves to be more dominate. When fighting against
your own policy attack_closest always result in a tie, while
other policy it could go either way.
